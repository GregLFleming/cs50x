Home Page

Welcome to my CS50 final project! This project's purpose is to create a space where Brazilian Jiu Jitsu athletes can learn new techniques, as well as contribute to a database of knowledge. This site is operated primarily through the navbar at the top. The Navbar can be used to filter techniques by both position and category.
In the future, I will add more functionality to the website such as a login system, an administrator system, page revision history, a voting system, custom user home pages ect. I believe, though, that the current site is enough to satisfy the scope of the CS50 final project.
